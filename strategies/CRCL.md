# CRCL

- Objective: reduce risk and raise cash while preserving upside during rebounds.
- Option style: covered call, 7-14 DTE, delta 0.15-0.22.
- Execution: sell on green days or intraday rebounds, avoid opening fresh calls on panic red days.
- If assigned: accept assignment as planned de-risk action.
- Guardrail: if headline risk spikes, shorten to 7-10 DTE and reduce size.
